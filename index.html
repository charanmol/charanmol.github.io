    • A short poem

    Be it red, or be it Pink
    my mind is obstinate,
    you're what I think.

    Wanders in fiction all day and night,
    I hear melodies and dream of us,
    and picture myself around roses of white.

    I hope the roses will be a story,
    one we'll contemplate for eternity,
    and I've found my glory,
    so I need no more scrutiny.
